About

30 years in the system. Then she changed it.

She didn't study leadership from the outside. She led from the inside — and now she brings everything she learned to the organizations brave enough to do the work.

The Rise

Labour negotiations at 2 AM. Leadership programs built from zero budget. Executives coached through mergers with 500 jobs on the line. She went from HR Director to CHRO to Senior Director at Unity Health Toronto.

The Reckoning

The higher she climbed, the smaller the room got. A Black woman in Canadian healthcare leadership — welcomed for her contributions, questioned for her presence. She reached the top. And then she asked herself a harder question than any board had asked her.

The Rebuilding

She didn't leave because she failed. She left because she succeeded — and realized she could do more from the outside than any system would let her do from within. AME Consulting is thirty years of hard-won leadership, finally unchained.

## ✏️ How to update your website
 
You can update your website anytime by chatting with Claude. No technical knowledge needed.
 
> 💚 **Nothing you do here is permanent or destructive.** GitHub automatically saves a full history of every change ever made to your website. If you ever don't like something, it can be undone in seconds — no matter how long ago the change was made. Think of it like an infinite undo button. Make changes with confidence.
 
### Before you start — one-time setup
 
The first time you use Claude to edit your website, you'll need to connect your GitHub account. You only ever do this once.
 
1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and sign in
2. In the left sidebar, click **Code**
3. Click **Connect GitHub**
4. Sign into your GitHub account when prompted
5. Click **Authorize** — that's it, you're connected
Once connected, you'll never need to do this again.

### Moving your Google Workspace billing to Google
 
Right now your email is billed through Network Solutions. You can switch to paying Google directly — same service, same price, just one less middleman.
 
1. Go to [admin.google.com](https://admin.google.com) and sign in with your `@ameconsulting.ca` email
2. Click **Billing** in the left menu, then **Subscriptions**
3. Go to **Account**, then **Reseller Management**
4. Find your subscription, click **Transfer**, and choose **Transfer to Google**
5. Enter a credit card — Google will bill you directly from that point on
Nothing changes when you do this. All your emails, contacts, and files stay exactly as they are.
 
> Full instructions from Google: [support.google.com/a/answer/7643791](https://support.google.com/a/answer/7643791)
